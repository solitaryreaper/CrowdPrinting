Download Test File

Quality and dependability are very important fir proper functioning of the largest distributed, coordinated, manufacturing system in the world. All 3D printers on the network need to first be properly classified based on their performance on the printtest file.

The print test file is a standard we apply across all 3D printers in order to properly understand their capabilities and limitations.

Download the Print Test File

Upon completion of the registration process, an empty pre-packaged, pre-paid box will be sent to your address. Simply print the test file, place it in the pre-paid box, and ship it back to CloudMake. Upon receipt, your test file will be inspected promptly and your account will be capable of viewing and accepting print orders in the queue.
